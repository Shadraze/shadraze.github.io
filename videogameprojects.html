Video Game Projects

Fighting Game with OpenGL

This is a simple 2D fighting game built from the ground up in OpenGL. I implemented collisions, animations, dabbled with shaders and also needed to code a miniature game engine to put it all together. I converted my 3D models into 2D spritesheets for the art assets. Suffice to say, this project helped me understand OpenGL and also how rendering graphics as a whole works.

Video Game AI and TROS

The Revenge of Sanosuke (TROS) is a game inspired by the popular game from 1999 "Little Fighter 2". The game had a huge community for modding their own characters into it, this flexibity is what made it so popular. TROS is also my undergraduate degree's 4th semester project. My focus on TROS was actually coming up with video game AI with higher difficulty. I had to put together the game too for that.

Disclaimer: I downloaded the free art assests from the aforementioned community's archives and music assets from other free sources.
